The ASCC Home Environment Sound Dataset

1. Introduction

This table shows the categories and the amount of audios of each category in the dataset samples.

The audios are collected from Google audioset [1], CHIME-HOME [2], SINS [3], Eating Sound Collection [4], Freesound [5].

The original audios from different sources have different sample rates, durations and channels. We unified them into 32kHz, 5 seconds, and mono. For some sources, the durations of those audios are smaller than 5 seconds. Their duration is kept in the original shape. Those audios whose duration is larger than 5 seconds are cut into 5-second segments.

After segmenting the audios, we manually verified the audios and removed the audios which have no sound event or unnoticeable sound event.
